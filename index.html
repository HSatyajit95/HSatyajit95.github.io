Research & Publications

Peer-reviewed publications from my MS research on assistive-device mechanism design at IIT Madras.

Journal Article

Design and Development of a Mobile Sit-to-Stand Assistive Device

Satyajit Halder et al. · Journal of The Institution of Engineers (India): Series C, Vol. 105, pp. 299–312 · 2024

Two sit-to-stand assistive device variants using four-bar linkage synthesis and double-parallelogram scissor mechanisms with linear actuators, validated on volunteers with a 45% reduction in ground reaction force. The device design is patented (2024).

Biomechanics Mechanism Design Assistive Devices
Conference Paper

Design and Development of a Sit-to-Stand Assistive Device

Satyajit Halder et al. · Machines, Mechanism and Robotics, Proceedings of iNaCoMM · 2019

Optimization-based formulation of sit-to-stand trajectories minimizing integrated joint torques across five actuated joints, with a simulation framework validating the assistive motion model.
